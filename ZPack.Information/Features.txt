-- Feature list for ZPack Vanilla --


Your vanilla, better than ever! - #ZPackVanilla
--------------------------------------------------

Main Features:
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Armour items are squarer, to better match worn models.
	Creeper is darker and leafier.
	Music discs are circular.
	Spear grips are coloured based on the material used for the spearhead.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unexposed.
	Nautilus armour items are round, based on the nautilus shell.
	Dyes are more detailed, and have their palettes removed.
	Cyan Glaazed Terracotta has its creeper face removed.
	Command blocks are symmetrical.
	Crafting & furnace toggles are consistent between versions.
	Brown bundles are acutally brown.
	Beacon beam is more pixel consistent.
	Tool and weapon items better than wooden have matching pommels.
	Item frame items better match placed block.
	Hopper & Cauldron items are more accurate to placed blocks.
	Brick & nether brick item better match crafted blocks.
	Redstone repeater and comparator items better match placed block.
	Chests & barrel have a fading darkness inside, instead of a flat colour.
	Spyglass & brush item textures better match their recipes.
	New Splash Texts.
	+Optional Paintings to replace unpacked & dennis!
	
	Many other small, but important changes.

Bedrock Edition Exclusives:
	Redstone torches are red and have no outline, to better match the other torches.
	Bed item colours better matches placed blocks.
	Silenced weak hit sound effect to match Java.
	
Java Edition Exclusives:
	Entity shadows are more pixel consistent.

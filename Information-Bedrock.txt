-- Information for ZPack Vanilla Plus (Pre-Release, Bedrock) --


Your vanilla, better than ever!
--------------------------------------------------
This texture pack is for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition, (1.21.90+)
Compatible with Vibrant Visuals.

Highlight Features:
	Generic UI tool and armour sprites are replaced with netherite ones (Armour bar, status effects, etc.)
	Food bar sprites are golden carrots instead of generic meat shanks.
	Creeper is based on a modern leaf texture.
	Raw copper + ores are no longer oxidised.
	Armour items are squarer.
	Leather item is darker.

Changelog:
	Added creeper skull.
	Added creeper spawn egg.
	Updated status effects sprites.

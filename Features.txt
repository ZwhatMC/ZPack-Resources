-- Feature list for ZPack Vanilla Plus, By ZwhatMC --


Your vanilla, better than ever! - 26.0.3: Cascaded Chests!
--------------------------------------------------

Main Features:
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Armour items are squarer, to match worn models.
	Generic interface equipment sprites and icons are replaced with netherite ones.
	Creeper is darker and leafier.
	Music discs are circular.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unexposed.
	Dyes are more detailed, and lack palettes.
	Nautilus armour items are round, based on the nautilus shell.
	Spear grips are coloured based on the material used for the spearhead.
	All chests have a cascading darkness inside, instead of pure black.
	Mojangles font is better vertically justified (Ascii only, no accented characters).
	Name tag has an iron loop to match its crafting recipe
	Command blocks are symmetrical and more logically textured (In my opinion.)

	Many other small, important changes.

Bedrock Edition Exclusives:
	Redstone torches are red and have no outline, to closer match Java redstone torches, and the other torch items.
	Bed item colour better matches placed block.
	

Java Edition Exclusives:
	Crafting and furnace recipe toggle buttons are simpler.

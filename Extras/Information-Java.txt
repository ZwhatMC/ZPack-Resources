Your vanilla, better than ever!
--------------------------------------------------
This texture pack is for Minecraft: Java Edition, (1.21.8)

Highlight Features:
	Generic UI tool and armour sprites are replaced with netherite ones.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unoxidised.
	Creeper + spawn egg is based on a modern leaf texture.
	Armour items are squarer to match worn models.
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Music records are circular.
	
	Many other small but important changes - Coming soon to YouTube!

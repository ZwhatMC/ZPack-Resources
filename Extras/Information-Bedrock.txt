Your vanilla, better than ever. Compatible with Vibrant Visuals!
--------------------------------------------------
This texture pack is for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition, (1.21.94.1)

Highlight Features:
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Armour items are squarer to match worn models.
	Generic UI & HUD equipment sprites are replaced with netherite ones.
	Creeper + spawn egg are based on a modern leaf texture.
	Music discs & records are circular.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unexposed.

	Many other small, very important changes.

Bedrock Edition Exclusives:
	Held bed items match placed colour.
		
Changelog:
	Moved copper equipment
	Added [default, exposed, weathered, oxidised]; lightning rods
	Updated [copper, iron, gold, diamond, netherite]; helmets

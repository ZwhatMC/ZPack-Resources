-- Information for ZPack Vanilla Plus Bedrock (Pre-Release) --


Your vanilla, better than ever, compatible with Vibrant Visuals!
--------------------------------------------------
This texture pack is for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition, (1.21.90+)

Highlight Features:
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Armour items are squarer to match worn models.
	Generic UI & hud equipment sprites are replaced with netherite ones.
	Creeper + spawn egg are based on a modern leaf texture.
	Music discs / records are circular.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unoxidised.

	Many other small, but very important changes.

Bedrock Edition Exclusives:
	Bed items match placed colour.

-- Feature list for ZPack Vanilla 26 --


Your vanilla, better than ever! - 26.13
--------------------------------------------------

Main Features:
	Hunger bar sprites are golden carrots.
	Armour items are squarer, to match worn models.
	Armour bar hud sprites are netherite.
	Creeper is darker and leafier.
	Music discs are circular.
	Raw copper, copper ores, and raw copper block are unexposed.
	Dyes are more detailed, and have their palettes removed.
	Nautilus armour items are round, based on the nautilus shell.
	Spear grips are coloured based on the material used for the spearhead.
	All chests have a fading darkness inside, instead of pure black.
	Cyan Terracotta has its creeper face removed.
	Mojangles font is better vertically justified.
	Command blocks are symmetrical.
	Brown bundles are brown!!
	Tool and weapon items better than wooden have matching pommels.
	Craftable toggles are consistent between versions.
	Item frame items are square.
	Hopper item is more accurate to placed block.
	Brick & nether brick item textures match their blocks better.
	
	Many other small, but important changes.

Bedrock Edition Exclusives:
	Redstone torches are red and have no outline, to closer match the other torches.
	Bed item colours better matches placed texture.
	
Java Edition Exclusives:
	Entity shadows are more pixel consistent.
	New Splash Texts.

Compatibility;
    Bedrock: Minimum engine version = 1.26.0, Capable of PBR (Vibrant Visuals)
    Java: Minimum pack format = 70.0, Maximum pack format = 100.0
    Updated for BE 26.10 & JE 26.1


ZPack Vanilla: Bedrock Installation Guide:
Cut/Copy the unzipped "Bedrock-xx.xx" folder.
Open File Explorer, and in the address bar, enter "%AppData%".
Navigate to; > "Roaming > Minecraft Bedrock > Users > Shared > games > com.mojang > resource_packs".
Paste the folder from your clipboard & restart Minecraft: Bedrock Edition.

All done, your vanilla is now better than ever!

--------------------------------------------------

ZPack Vanilla: Java Installation Guide:
Cut/Copy the unzipped "Java-xx.xx" folder.
Open Minecraft: Java Edition with your preferred launcher.
In Minecraft, select; Options, Resource Packs, "Open Pack Folder".
Inside the file browser window, paste the folder from your clipboard.

All done, now your vanilla is better than ever!
